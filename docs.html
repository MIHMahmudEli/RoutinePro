Welcome to RoutinePro

RoutinePro is more than just a schedule viewer—it's an intelligent course architect. It eliminates the frustration of conflicting class times by automatically finding every possible schedule combination based on your desired courses.

MISSION

Designed for AIUBians and university students worldwide, our goal is to turn the chaos of pre-registration into a streamlined, 5-minute process with professional-grade aesthetics.

Scenario Explorer

When you have multiple sections for each course, there might be hundreds of valid combinations. The Explorer lets you flip through them like a gallery.

Quick Navigation

Use the arrows in the header to switch between valid scenarios instantly.

Smart Sorting

Sort scenarios by "Minimum Waiting" to find the most compact schedules.

Focus Mode Popular

Standard routine grids are often cluttered with empty hours and weekends. Focus Mode intelligently crops the view to your specific needs.

  • Hides empty days (e.g., if you have no classes on Saturday, the day disappears).
  • Zooms into active hours (e.g., 08:00 AM to 02:00 PM) to maximize screen space.

Compact Mode

Perfect for high-density routines. When enabled, course titles are automatically abbreviated using their initials (e.g., "Computer Networks" → "CN").

Ramadan Mode

University schedules often shift during the holy month of Ramadan. Instead of manually re-editing your times, enable Ramadan Mode to automatically shift all class blocks to the holiday schedule.

How it works

RoutinePro uses a pre-synchronized global database to map standard slots (e.g., 08:00-09:30) to their Ramadan equivalent (e.g., 08:30-09:40).

Portal Sync
